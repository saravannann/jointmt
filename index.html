About Us

Thenmozhi Memorial Trust is the Non Profitable Organization.

At Thenmozhi Memorial Trust, we believe that every second counts. Our mission is built on the pillars of early detection, financial support for treatments, and emotional resilience. Based in the heart of Chennai, we bridge the gap between world-class care and those who need it most. From weekend volunteering drives to massive charity fundraisers, we are turning the tide against cancer—one life at a time.
