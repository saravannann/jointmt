About Us

Thenmozhi Memorial Trust is the Non Profitable Organization.

Thenmozhi Memorial Trust was instituted in the loving memory of Mrs. Thenmozhi Arokia Mary in 2014. The trust aims to be an enabling platform that brings like-minded youngsters together to work towards making a difference in the lives of less fortunate cancer patients. TMT's volunteers comprises of thoughtful youngsters who vow to make their presence felt amongst the less fortunate and aim to achieve awareness for the disease. Their work does not stop with monetary means and medicines but, surpasses all that. They give patients the 'Gift of Time' . Our volunteers regularly visit hospices and homes of people battling with cancer to show their solidarity. TMT has been able to touch the lives of over 13000+ terminally ill patients since 2014 through such activities.
